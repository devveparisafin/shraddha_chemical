// Product Overview

What Is Electronic Grade Al₂O₃?

A precisely manufactured form of aluminium oxide meeting the stringent purity and particle size demands of the electronics and semiconductor industry.

Chemical Formula
Al2O3
Dialuminium Trioxide · Corundum · Alumina
Alumina Corundum α-Al₂O₃ Aluminum Oxide Electronic Alumina

Electronic grade aluminium oxide is a high-purity, precisely controlled powder or ceramic material produced to meet the exacting standards of the semiconductor, LED, power electronics, and advanced substrate industries.

Unlike industrial-grade alumina, electronic grade Al₂O₃ undergoes rigorous purification processes to achieve purity levels of 99.9% to 99.999%, with tightly controlled particle size distributions, minimal ionic contamination, and extremely low levels of trace metals that could compromise electronic device performance.

Available in multiple crystallographic phases (α, γ, θ), particle morphologies, and surface area specifications to suit substrate manufacturing, polishing, coating, and dielectric applications.

We manufacture and supply electronic grade aluminium oxide in batch quantities from laboratory scale to multi-tonne industrial volumes, with full traceability and certificate of analysis (CoA) for every lot.
