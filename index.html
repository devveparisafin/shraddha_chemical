About Us

Built on Precision.
Driven by Purpose.

Founded to serve India's semiconductor manufacturing sector with world-class chemical inputs — we're building it right from the ground up.

The semiconductor industry demands nothing less than perfection — impurities measured in parts per billion, consistency across every batch, and reliability in every delivery. Shraddha Chemical was born from this understanding.

We are a newly founded company focused on a carefully chosen portfolio: Copper Sulfate, Aluminium Oxide, and Colloidal Silica — three materials that sit at the heart of semiconductor fabrication and precision electronics manufacturing.

We are currently in the process verification phase. Every step we take is deliberate — because in our industry, getting it right matters far more than getting there fast.
